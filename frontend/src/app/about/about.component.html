CyLiis

Thinking beyond the CODE. Engineering beyond the ROBOT.

CyLiis represents the wish of passionate students to try the hands-on learning experience in the challenge made by “Natie prin educatie” through the First Tech Challenge program, starting with the Relic Recovery season. CyLiis is a complex experience, a place where we transform ideas into robots, come up with ingenious solutions for technical problems, draw 3D models, print prototypes using 3D printers, build automatic systems, programming and train for competitions and demos. We always organize activities in which we give back what we won after being part of the CyLiis family. We learned that “Robotics unites Romania” is not just a slogan, it is an axiom.
